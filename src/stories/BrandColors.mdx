## Usage notes

- Deep Navy: primary text, labels, and table text.
- Royal Blue: headings, accents, and links.
- Golden Yellow: primary Button gradient and highlights.
- Red Orange: primary Button gradient, required marks, and error accents.
- White: page background and card surfaces.
